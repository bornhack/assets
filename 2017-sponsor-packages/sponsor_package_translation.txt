BornHack is a unique event in Denmark, where experts from many different fields meet. Therefore it is a good possibility to sit in the frontrow when new technology is discussed, experimented with.

BornHack is also a fantastic place for teambuilding and expanding your employees and colleagues competences, there is ample opportunity to deepen and broaden knowledge, get to know each other and have a lot of fun.

If you have a product to sell, bring your swag and discount codes and distribute them among our attendees.
